Opening Idea

There is only one real tech skill:

“Where is the file?”

What People Say

Common wrong answers

  • “It’s in Microsoft Word”
  • “It’s on the computer”
  • “It’s in Google”
  • “It’s in the cloud”
  • “It’s saved”

More Wrong Answers

The file-lost starter pack

  • “It’s in my email”
  • “It’s on my desktop”
  • “I just had it open”
  • “It auto-saved”
  • “It’s on my other computer”

Why This Fails

Why vague answers break confidence

  • You do not know the actual location
  • You do not know the real file name
  • You do not know how many copies exist
  • You do not know which version is correct

The Correct Answer

What control sounds like

“It’s in this folder, on this system, with this exact filename, and I know where else it exists.”

One file. Known location.
